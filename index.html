WELCOME TO THE GTSC!

PERSON/ANIMAL WHO HAS VISITED THIS SITE SINCE 2001 (GIVE OR TAKE A FEW 50K FOR WHEN I VISITED MYSELF TO TAKE IN THE SHEER BEAUTY OF THE SITE)

If you've never been here before, this is yet another Gran Turismo fansite - except one of the older ones (grey hairs have been dyed). I've had this place online in various other designs and locations since 2001 - so it's not quite a spring onion chicken! For this latest relaunch i've decided that sensible is too boring, so have rewritten a lot on all the pages to add my personal sense of humour, like on my other websites. I find it funny, so thats all that matters. If you do too then either someone is paying you to, or you're tuned into the same brainwave altering frequency as i am.

I will get you to note however, that ALL the information on the site (car lists, track info etc) is as accurate as the majority of the other lists out there on the interwebs, and are generally 99% free of my 'humour'. I simply 'humourfied all the other info so that you stay on the site longer and read more. :) No boring bits. Except for the bits you find boring.

Each time i update the site i'll try use a new image in the main logo, only with photos i've taken in GT5/GT6. The Facebook 'Like' box is at the bottom of this page. Head over and 'Like' it! It likes being clicked. Don't you like being clicked too? It's clicktacular fun!
